# Community FAQ

## Getting Started
**Q: How do I join the community?**
A: Click the verification link in #welcome and follow the steps. You'll get access within a few minutes.

**Q: Where can I find the rules?**
A: Community rules are pinned in #rules. Please read them before posting.

## Support
**Q: How do I report a bug?**
A: Use the #bug-reports channel and include steps to reproduce, your OS, and screenshots if possible.

**Q: How do I contact a moderator?**
A: Open a ticket in #support or DM any online moderator (they have the Moderator role).

## Account & Access
**Q: I lost access to my account. What do I do?**
A: Contact an admin in #support with your username and approximate join date.

**Q: Can I change my nickname?**
A: Yes! Right-click your name → Edit Server Profile → set a nickname.

## The Covenant & Human Tech

**What is The Covenant?**
The Covenant is a framework for culture, contribution, and governance within the human.tech ecosystem. It defines how human-aligned technologies are developed and stewarded.

**How should The Covenant be referenced?**

* Formal: “The Covenant of Humanistic Technologies”
* Shorthand: “The Covenant of Human Tech”
* Short: “The Covenant” (used for the DAO or document)

**What is Human Tech?**
Human Tech refers to the broader category of practices and innovations guided by The Covenant’s design philosophy.

**What is human.tech?**
human.tech refers specifically to the infrastructure and ecosystem built according to The Covenant’s principles.

## NFTs

**What is the Signatory NFT?**
A limited-edition NFT minted after signing The Covenant and completing human verification.

**Why did my NFT fail to mint?**
This happens because the Signatory NFT supply is fully minted (256 per edition, 512 total). You can still submit an artifact and receive an NFT if approved.

**Can NFTs be transferred or sold?**
Yes. Covenant NFTs are transferable and can be traded.

**Will the Signatory NFT supply increase?**
No. The supply is fixed and will not be expanded.

## Artifacts

**What are artifacts?**
Artifacts are submissions that represent meaningful human contributions—such as essays, research, art, or open-source work.

**Do I have to submit an artifact?**
No. Submitting an artifact is optional. You can participate after verification without submitting immediately.

**How many artifacts can I submit?**
Currently, one artifact per user.

**What makes a strong artifact?**
Strong artifacts:

* Align with Covenant principles
* Demonstrate originality and understanding
* Provide clear value or impact
* Are well-developed and intentional

**What disqualifies an artifact?**

* Misalignment with Covenant principles
* Generic or primarily AI-generated content
* Incomplete or unclear submissions
* Harmful or irrelevant content

**Can AI-assisted work be submitted?**
Yes, but the creator must clearly understand and stand behind the work. Purely AI-generated content is unlikely to be approved.

**How long does approval take?**
Typically a few weeks to up to three months, depending on volume and review depth.

**Can I resubmit if rejected?**
Not yet. Resubmissions may be opened after all initial reviews are completed.
