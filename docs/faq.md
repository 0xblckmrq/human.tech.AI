# Community FAQ

## Getting Started
**Q: How do I join the community?**
A: Click the verification link in #welcome and follow the steps. You'll get access within a few minutes.

**Q: Where can I find the rules?**
A: Community rules are pinned in #rules. Please read them before posting.

## Support
**Q: How do I report a bug?**
A: Use the #bug-reports channel and include steps to reproduce, your OS, and screenshots if possible.

**Q: How do I contact a moderator?**
A: Open a ticket in #support or DM any online moderator (they have the Moderator role).

## Account & Access
**Q: I lost access to my account. What do I do?**
A: Contact an admin in #support with your username and approximate join date.

**Q: Can I change my nickname?**
A: Yes! Right-click your name → Edit Server Profile → set a nickname.
